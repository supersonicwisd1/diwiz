About

DIWIZ School is keen on building up student of high standard. We impact educationally, morally, and spiritually. We build up student that lives in the fear of God.

What we offer

DIWIZ Schools can only succeed in its mission of providing teaching excellence to fulfil your child’s potential by offering the best possible learning environment, with the most effective learning tools and facilities.

The classroom environment at DIWIZ Schools allows your child’s educators to give them the time and attention that they need in order to succeed. We believe in keeping class numbers low to maximum learning potential.

Our Facilities

Each student has access to the best possible learning technologies, as well having guest classes from industry professionals. We believe this better prepares your child for their careers or own businesses.

We believe in equipping your children with the skills to be able to learn for the rest of their lives! By instilling in them a method of analytical thinking, we believe they will be able to be independent thinkers and be high performers in their chosen careers.

Our Teachers

DIWIZ teachers play an indispensable role in the lives of the students equipping them with the knowledge ans skills required for career and life. They are equipped to develop trusting, productive relationship with students.

DIWIZ teachers are patient, caring, kind and dedicated to the inculcation of good morals to the students to build thwm to be high esteemed citizens of the world at large
